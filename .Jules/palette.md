## 2025-05-15 - Clarifying Technical Acronyms and Enhancing Screen Reader Support

**Learning:** Technical domain-specific acronyms (like AAMVA's DCG, DAC, DCS) can create cognitive friction for users unfamiliar with the standard. Additionally, icon-only buttons in Streamlit require the `help` parameter to provide an accessible label for screen readers.

**Action:** Always provide descriptive tooltips (`help` parameter in Streamlit) for technical acronyms and icon-only buttons. Ensure decorative or informative images (like flags) have appropriate `alt` text for accessibility.
