## 2025-04-28 - [Form Accessibility and Feedback]
**Learning:** In technical applications like AAMVA barcode generators, cryptic industry-standard acronyms (e.g., DCG, DAC) are a significant UX barrier. Adding descriptive tooltips (`help` in Streamlit) and increasing label contrast significantly improves usability and accessibility.
**Action:** Always check for technical jargon in forms and provide clear, contextual help. Ensure label contrast meets accessibility standards in custom-themed interfaces.
