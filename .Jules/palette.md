## 2025-05-15 - [AAMVA Technical Accessibility]
**Learning:** Technical field acronyms (like AAMVA codes) are major barriers for non-technical users. High-contrast labels (0.9 opacity on dark backgrounds) significantly improve readability over standard muted labels (0.6 opacity) in custom "Liquid Glass" themes.
**Action:** Always provide descriptive tooltips for technical acronyms and prioritize high-contrast text ratios in custom styled Streamlit applications to ensure accessibility and clarity.
