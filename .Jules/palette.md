## 2025-05-15 - [AAMVA Technical Field Clarity]
**Learning:** In technical domains like AAMVA identity generation, cryptic field codes (DCG, DAC, etc.) are a significant barrier to usability. Providing human-readable tooltips via `help` parameters in Streamlit is a low-effort, high-impact way to improve both UX and accessibility.
**Action:** Always look for domain-specific acronyms in forms and provide clear, descriptive tooltips or help text to guide non-expert users.
