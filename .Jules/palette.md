## 2025-05-15 - Enhancing Cryptic Field Labels with Tooltips
**Learning:** In technical interfaces like AAMVA barcode generators, users often face cryptic three-letter field codes (DCG, DAC, etc.). Providing localized tooltips via the `help` parameter in Streamlit significantly improves accessibility and self-documentation without cluttering the UI.
**Action:** Always provide descriptive tooltips for technical acronyms or industry-specific field codes. Condense localized dictionary entries to single lines to maintain a clean codebase and stay within line-count limits for UX-focused PRs.
